=============================================================================
              REXMO PHOTOGRAPHY - MASTER IMAGE CURATION GUIDE
=============================================================================

GLOBAL AESTHETIC RULES FOR ALL IMAGES:
1. Lighting: Favor "Chiaroscuro" (high contrast between light and shadow). Avoid flat, overly bright, or overly airy "light and fine" editing.
2. Color Grading: True-to-life editorial colors, deep rich blacks, and warm skin tones. Mixed with high-grain black-and-white analog shots.
3. Composition: Architectural framing, negative space, and unscripted motion (blurry dresses, wind-swept hair, genuine laughter).
4. Consistency: Ensure all photos look like they were graded by the exact same artist.

- Location: Next to the Analog text
- Aspect Ratio: 4:5 (Vertical)
- Content: An actual behind-the-scenes shot of a vintage 35mm or medium format film camera (like a Leica, Hasselblad, or Contax) resting on a textured table, or a photographer's hands loading film. 

[Curated Collections Grid]
- Aspect Ratio: 3:4 (Vertical) for all four cards
- Card 1 (Weddings): An epic, editorial couple portrait. Think Vogue-style posing, perhaps walking away looking back over the shoulder.
- Card 2 (Maternity): An elegant, silhouetted maternity shot against a window, or a high-fashion studio maternity portrait in black-and-white.
- Card 3 (Portraits): A fierce, confident solo portrait. Direct eye contact with the camera, sharp studio lighting.
- Card 4 (Events): A candid, joyous moment. Clinking champagne glasses, guests laughing in evening wear under dim ambient light.

[Selected Frames - Staggered 3-Column Gallery]
- Aspect Ratio: All 4:5 (Vertical)
- Image 1 (Left): A wide environmental portrait (e.g., a couple tiny in a massive landscape or cityscape).
- Image 2 (Center - Pushed Down): A highly emotional, close-up candid (e.g., a tear falling, a genuine mid-laugh embrace).
- Image 3 (Right - Slight offset): A sharp, high-fashion detail (e.g., a blurred motion shot of a dress spinning, or macro jewelry).

[Cinematic Motion Video Placeholder]
- Aspect Ratio: 21:9 (Ultra-wide Cinema format)
- Content: If not an actual video, use a still frame that looks exactly like a movie scene. Heavy film grain, letterboxed (black bars on top and bottom), perhaps a shot of a vintage car driving away or a couple dancing under warm tungsten string lights.

[Tangible Legacies - Albums]
- Aspect Ratio: 4:5 (Vertical)
- Content: A highly curated, tactile product shot. A beautiful, thick Italian leather photo album resting on a linen or stone surface, perhaps with a cup of black coffee or scattered analog film negatives next to it. Soft, natural window light.
