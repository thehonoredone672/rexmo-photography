FOUNDER

Francis Jeya Balan

The Vision Behind the Legacy

Francis Jeya Balan founded Rexmo in 1992 with a deep passion for wedding photography, dedicating himself to documenting life’s most meaningful celebrations with elegance and emotion.

His commitment to excellence, discipline, and artistic integrity laid a strong foundation for the brand, Rexmo. Though he is no longer with us, his vision for timeless and refined wedding storytelling continues to inspire every frame we create.

Rexmo stands today as a tribute to his legacy—classic, graceful, and enduring.

CREATIVE DIRECTOR

Jesley Frantin

Carrying forward the legacy, Jesley Frantin leads Rexmo with a modern artistic vision while preserving its classic elegance.

As a passionate wedding photographer, he approaches each wedding as a bespoke story—thoughtfully composed, emotionally rich, and crafted with precision.

Under his direction, Rexmo continues to evolve while honoring the values on which it was built.
