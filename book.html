Our Poetic Process

01

The Inquiry & Dossier Submission

Submit your extensive narrative dossier below. Tell us about your desired aesthetic, vision, and event planner. If available, our studio manager connects within 48 hours.

02

The Curation Consultation

A virtual or in-person meeting over coffee or champagne. We discuss aesthetic synergy, timeline flow, and how we unobtrusively curate frames without breaking your event's rhythm.

03

The Cinematic Day

Our fully styled principal photographers arrive prepared. We balance immaculate, composed editorial portraits with raw photojournalistic coverage of true, unscripted emotion.

04

Legacy Delivery & Archiving

Preview frames arrive within 72 hours. Your high-resolution, meticulously mastered gallery is delivered within 8-10 weeks via a premium visual portal. Italy-handcrafted heirloom albums are curated.
